My experience learning to code

Read time: 5 minutes

As an International Student where it is hot all year I never exprience summer holiday. I am quite shocked to learn that it is so looonggggg. My number one concern is wasting all the time I have given doing something that is not beneficial for me. Until...

!!! Couch to Coder 2024!!!

I got an email about the programme but the last time I learnt to code is probably 6 years ago and I remember not enjoying it that much. But having an activity is better than wasting my time away, right?

"Your future self will thank you" - me to myself, trying to convince myself to sign up

Long story short I not only sign up for this programme but a bunch of other programme to fill my summer holiday but today I am going to walk you through my Coch to Coder learning experience.

First Week : woah, I actually kind of enjoy that lesson.

Second Week : Maybe it is not that bad after all, I can finish this.

Third Week : uh oh I am going on a holiday, what if I missed the deadline.

Forth Week : Still on Holiday there is no way I am finishing all this.

A few days after the Fourth Week : Finally back, got a fever but determine to finish this up once and for all.

Fast Forward to final task: Oh no! I have no idea what to build or what to do. I should pribably do something fun to catch people's attention. BUT that's not too realistic not is it?

Lack of idea is very demotivating but I need to submit something

Let's just write about the whole experience (in short because well someone decide to procrastinate and now there is not enough time to come up with something grand)

There it is my whole experience for the past month trying to complete this programme.
